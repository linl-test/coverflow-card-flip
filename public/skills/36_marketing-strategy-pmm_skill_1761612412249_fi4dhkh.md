## About this skill

The Marketing Strategy PMM skill provides a comprehensive playbook for B2B SaaS product marketing, focusing on positioning, go-to-market strategies, and competitive intelligence. It includes frameworks like the April Dunford positioning methodology, launch playbooks, and international market entry guides, helping product marketing managers effectively plan product launches, create impactful messaging, and analyze competitor strategies.
