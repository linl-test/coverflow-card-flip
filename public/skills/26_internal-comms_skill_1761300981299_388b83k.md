- Author: Anthropic

## About this skill

Professional internal communication toolkit with company-approved templates and guidelines for multiple communication types. Create polished 3P updates (Progress, Plans, Problems) following standard team update formats. Write engaging company newsletters with appropriate tone and structure. Draft clear FAQ responses that address common questions effectively. Prepare comprehensive status reports, leadership updates, and project updates that communicate key information concisely. Document incident reports with proper detail and appropriate urgency. Each communication type includes specific formatting guidelines, tone recommendations, and content gathering strategies. Ensures consistency across all internal communications while maintaining professional standards and company voice.
